While locked, the seeker still gets size, price, environment, compliance and reviews — everything needed to choose — but nothing that lets the two parties skip the platform.

Why it works this way

Disintermediation — parties connecting off-platform — is the biggest risk to a marketplace. By revealing identity and exact location only after both sides commit through SpaceLink, the platform stays in the loop for the transaction, payments and trust, without making browsing any harder.
